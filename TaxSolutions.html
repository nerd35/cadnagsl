TAX SOLUTION



We simply enable you not to have Tax related issues of any kind. Our liaison guides you to the best filing options (FIRS & LIRS). Avoid double taxation and stay compliant with tax laws! This is also a way to deliver quality services to taxpayers in partnership with all stakeholders and make taxation the pivot of national development. We also handle Advisory on Federal and state taxes, VAT certificate, tax reconciliation, tax clearance and certificate, etc.

Tax always comes into play in almost every facet of the business. To avoid surprise losses related to tax, tax advice needs to be sought before you enter into business transactions. Hence, tax planning is best developed in advance. However, certain transactions may have been undertaken without proper tax planning or even without any tax planning at all.

A tax Liaison is an expert with advanced training and knowledge of tax law. The services of a tax advisor are usually retained in order to minimize tax payable while remaining compliant with the law in complicated financial situations.

Again, Tax reconciliation is usually effective with business firms and organization, it’s a collection of all unpaid taxes, here, and the firm is expected to pay the rough run down of tax issued to it. The Process involves Statement of Affairs and Audit Account as well as Late Return Payments and Tax Returns respectively.

We ensure the services of acquiring your TAX & VAT certificates are carried out promptly and efficiently.

NB: People often Neglect, Fear and Ignore TAXES, because they either assume they would not be held accountable due to poor enforcement, or the tax deductions are lofty, or simply do not understand how it works or work around it. These therefore lead to accumulation. This is usually not healthy because it no longer becomes a Stipend but an Expense. Everyone, especially organizations and firms are specially advice to consult tax liaisons to avoid issues.
